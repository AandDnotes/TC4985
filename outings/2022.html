First Outing - 17th January

Under a clear blue sky and with little wind, I took the TC out for the first trip of the year. I only did 17 miles; up through Ashurst to Partridge Green and back down through Henfield. It took a while for the engine to warm up (there is no thermostat fitted), but it ran well. The only problem was the sun in my eyes on the way back south.
